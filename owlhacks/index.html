Owl Hacks 2017
Foothill College Computer Science and Inventor's clubs are putting on a hackathon!

What's a hackathon?
Wikipedia has a good general explanation of what a hackathon is.

Where will it be held?
Room 4223, foothill college main campus (near the STEM center).
Here's a map which should get you to the right place.

And when?
10 AM to 6 PM Friday, February 17th, and continuing from 10 AM to 6 PM February 18th. Note that this is not a formal event, and you are welcome to drop in and leave as you please. Judging of projects will take place towards the end of Saturday, so make sure to be there if you want to win any prizes.

Who can attend?
Anybody interested in hanging out for a weekend and making cool stuff.

Will there be food?
Yes! There will rolls for lunch and pizza for dinner on both days. (And snacks throughout the day, in case you get peckish.)

What else should I bring?
Yourself. Your brain. Whatever computers/hardware you plan on hacking with. Note that this is not an overnight hackathon, and so sleeping bags are NOT needed.

And all of this is free?!
Yes. It will be paid for out of the club's budget.
